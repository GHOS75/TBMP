Who are we?

The Big Match Project team of Martin Goodman and Dean Rimmer have a wealth of experience in the sporting world. We aim to bring this experience, and the experiences of others to light so that we may help young athletes, parent and sports facilitators through the challenges that are inevitably faced.

Our vision is to create a mentorship platform that makes vital information readily available and accessible to child, parent and coach that assists them to better understanding and deal with issues, challenges and problems that occur in achieving oneâ€™s true potential.
